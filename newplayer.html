Team History

The team was founded in 2015 by Luis Garza, an alumni and former teacher at Don Bosco Technical Institute. With a group of devoted students, he formed the first iteration of the team which debuted at the 2015 Los Angeles Regional and ranked 30th out of 66 teams. He devised a plan that would guarantee the teams improvement over the course of 5 years, eventually leading them to participate in a Regional Final. In three years we achieved this goal and a year later we went even further.

School Info

Don Bosco Technical Institute is an all-boys Catholic High School whose goal is to create the next generation of engineers. It has six prestigious engineering courses ranging from Materials Science, Media Arts, Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Architecture, and Biomedical Sciences.
