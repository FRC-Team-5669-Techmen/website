What is FRC?

FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) is a robotics competition held in countries all around the globe. For the first 6 weeks of every year, teams design, manufacture, and program robots to complete the challenge given by FIRST. They then compete against each other for recognition, awards, and scholarships.
