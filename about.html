Team History

The team began as a rookie in 2015 by Luis Garza, a alumni and current teacher at Don Bosco Technical Institute. The school educates the students in different engineering professions such as civil engineering, mechanical engineering, material science, media arts, computer science, electrical engineering, and biomedical engineering. He and a group of devoted students formed the first iteration of the team who first competed in 2015 Los Angeles Regional and ranked 30 out of 66 teams. He devised a plan that guarantees that the team will consistently improve in the course of 5 years and will eventually lead the team to participate in a Regional Final. In three years we achieved this goal and a year later we went even further.
